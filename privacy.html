Saha Kulisi - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Saha Kulisi is a football content brand that produces and publishes short videos to our own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). This Privacy Policy explains how our internal publishing tool handles data when it connects to these platforms.

Who this applies to

Our publishing tool is used only by the Saha Kulisi team to post content to our own Saha Kulisi accounts. We do not offer this tool to third parties and we do not collect data from any other users.

Information we access

When we authorize the tool with TikTok via TikTok Login, we access only basic account information (user.info.basic) to confirm the correct account, and video upload permission (video.upload) to upload our own finished videos to the Saha Kulisi TikTok account as drafts, which we then review and publish from the TikTok app. We do not access followers, private messages, or any personal data of viewers.

How we use the information

The access token and account information are used solely to upload Saha Kulisi videos to our own account. Access tokens are stored locally and securely and are never shared with third parties.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data obtained through TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube APIs with third parties. Data is used only for our own content publishing.

Data retention

Access tokens are kept only as long as needed and can be revoked at any time from the platform's connected-apps settings. We do not store viewer data.

Content

All video content we publish uses royalty-free, licensed, or public-domain material. We do not upload third-party copyrighted footage.

Contact
